How We Help?

Whether you are looking forward to expand your team or need a help for a specialized project because your hands are full, you need an affordable and highly motivated talent to get the job done for you. Efficient hiring comes with many challenges, including layers of verification and series of interviews. Traditional freelancing comes with comprise, either with budget or quality and time. NeedSpot solves the problem by bringing together businesses owners with specialized project needs and highly qualified students with latest technological knowledge who can quickly execute on these critical projects within budget.

Why NeedSpot?

By accelerating the hiring process we enable startups to onboard someone they can trust, who has the right mindset and skills required to achieve their company goals without compromising the quality of service or product. At NeedSpot our customers save time, money and get what they need when they need it. Without extensive search and verification, hiring affordable talent becomes easier and faster for employers enabling Students to earn, build qualifications as well as grow their social and professional network.

About the Founder
Sarah Furqan is an aspiring entrepreneur, a Mum in Tech and advocate for women empowerment as well as equity diversity and inclusion. Sarah has over a decade of experience in app and software development. She is an international student, studying Masters of Management, Innovation and Entrepreneurship. Sara had a chance to connect with several entrepreneurs to study further the challenges that comes with turning an idea into a product or digital service and how not having a high performance team affect the journey of startup or vice versa. She believes that small to medium size business and students can help each other and bring positive outcomes for each other. However, it has to be an effective hiring process. With NeedSpot her vision is to bring those two groups together.
